Gameplay and game features

This game includes several sections for gamers, by selecting each of them, you can experience different sections and game models of this game. Battle Royale, multiplayer and rank modes have different modes that players can play depending on the situation. Choose one and play it yourself, for example in Battle Royale mode a hundred people land on an island and the last group or person who survives wins, but in Multiplayer and Rank mode only two groups in different maps in Different modes fight each other.In addition to the game model, you can choose different maps to run the game, which adds to the charm of the game, the game begins with competition between two teams of 5 people and at the beginning of the game a few seconds COOL DOWN to prepare players It is intended that the maps used in this game were used in other versions of the game that were made for WINDOWS platforms and game consoles, and if you are a fan of this game You will definitely notice this.


Check the multiplayer mode and rank of Call of Duty Mobile

Fold and get a good score. Of course, these capabilities will be provided to you when, firstly, your level has reached a sufficient level and secondly, in the game, without being killed, you have gained enough points to activate, for example, choppers, missiles and other capabilities.
