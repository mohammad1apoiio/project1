Rainbow Six Siege

The Rainbow Six series was one of Ubisoft's most popular games and the Tom Clancy series, and it was strange why we haven't seen the release of a new version in recent years. Although a new version was introduced and then canceled, it was finally the turn of this popular series and Siege was introduced during E3 2014. During this time and until the release of the game, Ubisoft practically did not make extensive publicity for Siege so that the game would be overshadowed by the great works of Ubisoft. Unaware that Siege is one of the best and highest quality Ubisoft games in recent years.
